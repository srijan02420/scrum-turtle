Scrum

Agile - its a methodology which adhere to 4 values and 12 principles for iterative and evolving product development Scrum - its one of the framework which follow agile methodology Scrum has 3 artifact, 5 events and 3 actors

Artifacts Product Backlog : It is ordered list of stories/feature, bug, debts those needed to be worked by scrum team Sprint backlog : It is the list of items taken by dev team to be worked in current sprint Product Increment : it is the work completed by team by end of sprint

Events Sprint : it is one timebox in scrum process. Sprint Planning : Dev team decide the list of task those will be taken in current sprint Daily scrum : It is essential for dev team to give their updates,what they have done,what will they do and if there is any blocker Sprint review : it will attended by stakeholders,scrum team will show what they have completed in sprint Sprint retrospective : Scrum team will discuss about their learning from current sprint and how will they improve Roles Product Owner : He is responsible of product backlog and ordering of items Scrum master : He is responsible for smooth functioning of the scrum and unblocking team Development team - these are the ground soldiers working with scrum master and product owner to achieve the decided sprint goal
